Class: BookPreview

BookPreview()

`book-preview` is a custom Web Component that displays a book preview card. It shows the book's image, title, and author inside a styled button element. Attributes: - `image`: The URL of the book's cover image. - `title`: The title of the book. - `author`: The author's name. - `id`: The unique identifier of the book (used in `data-preview` attribute).
